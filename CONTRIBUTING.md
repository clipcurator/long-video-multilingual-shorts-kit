# Contributing

This repository is maintained as a neutral practical resource. Contributions should improve clarity, usefulness, or review quality.

## Good Contributions

- Better templates
- Clearer checklists
- More realistic examples
- Additional decision criteria
- Notes that help teams avoid repeated mistakes

## Editorial Rules

- Keep the tone neutral and knowledge-first.
- Do not turn the repository into an advertisement.
- Avoid absolute claims such as "best", "guaranteed", or "number one".
- If a tool is mentioned, explain its fit and boundary.
- Prefer short paragraphs and copy-ready templates.
